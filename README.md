## Networking Project

https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c0f0152a-16e3-471d-8234-832f54dbb844

This project was created alongside a business plan I developed in my final year of university. It was my first attempt at building a networking system that allowed players to connect and play together over both LAN and online networks.

As part of the project, I also developed a character selection system, as well as an account creation system that synchronised player information across connected clients. In addition, I successfully integrated the project with Steam services to support online functionality and connectivity.
